Home FAQ

What Types of Events Do You Manage?

We specialize in a wide range of events including weddings, receptions, corporate conferences, theme parties, birthday celebrations, cultural events, and college festivals. Whatever your occasion, we have the expertise to make it memorable and flawless.

Weddings Corporate Parties

How Far in Advance Should I Book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events. For corporate events and smaller celebrations, 2-3 months notice is ideal. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

Pro Tip: Book early to secure your preferred date and vendors!

Do You Provide Services Outside Your City?

Yes! We provide event management services across India. Additional travel and accommodation charges may apply for outstation events. Contact us to discuss your specific location requirements.

What's Included in Your Packages?

Our packages vary by tier:

  • Basic: Planning and decoration
  • Standard: + Photography and catering coordination
  • Premium: Everything + videography, advanced decor, VIP management

All packages are fully customizable to your needs.

Can I Customize My Event Package?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable packages tailored to your requirements and budget. Mix and match services from different packages or request additional services. Your vision is our priority.

What's Your Payment Structure?

Our payment structure is designed for your convenience:

30% Advance to confirm booking
40% One month before event
30% After event completion

We accept bank transfers, UPI, and credit/debit cards. Custom payment plans available for large events.

How Does the Planning Process Work?

Our streamlined planning process ensures a stress-free experience:

  1. Initial Consultation: We discuss your vision, preferences, and budget
  2. Detailed Planning: Create comprehensive plan including theme, venue, vendors, and timeline
  3. Regular Updates: Scheduled meetings to keep you informed
  4. Event Execution: Our team handles all logistics on the day

Do You Help With Venue Selection?

Yes! We have partnerships with premium venues across multiple cities. We'll recommend venues based on your requirements, guest count, budget, and event type. We can also work with your chosen venue.

Do You Work With External Vendors?

Yes, we coordinate with your preferred vendors. We also have a network of 200+ trusted, verified vendors for catering, photography, decoration, and entertainment. We ensure all vendors meet our quality standards.
